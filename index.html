Knowledge Base

Common questions answered

The solar enhancement system is designed with thermal mass storage that maintains optimal temperatures (38-42¬∞C) for up to 48 hours without direct sunlight. Additionally, the biological processes in the septic tank and wetland continue to function effectively even during temporary temperature fluctuations. The nano-silver filter operates independently of solar conditions.

Yes! Our modular design allows for retrofitting to most standard septic tank configurations. The solar enhancement can be added as an external attachment, and the nano-filter is designed to be installed in-line with existing outlet plumbing. The constructed wetland requires additional land area but can be integrated with your current drainage setup.

The nano-silver activated carbon filter typically requires replacement every 18-24 months, depending on household usage and water quality. This is a DIY-friendly process that takes approximately 30 minutes. The filter media is locally available and can be replaced at minimal cost ($25-40). We provide detailed installation guides and video tutorials.

The wetland is designed for low-maintenance operation. Key maintenance tasks include: harvesting mature plants every 6-12 months (which can be composted or used as mulch), checking inlet/outlet for clogs quarterly, and monitoring plant health. The gravel bed typically does not require replacement if properly maintained. Most tasks can be completed in under 1 hour per month.

Absolutely. Our three-stage treatment train achieves WHO standards for unrestricted irrigation, including food crops. The system removes 99.999% of pathogens, making the output water safe for all agricultural applications. However, we recommend applying water to soil rather than directly onto edible plant parts as a best practice.

The system is designed with overflow protection and elevated components to handle heavy rainfall. The constructed wetland can temporarily absorb excess water volume, and all critical electrical components are weather-sealed. In extreme flood conditions, the system can be isolated to prevent backflow. We recommend elevated installation for flood-prone areas.
